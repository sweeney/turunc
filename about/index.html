About the apartment 

 

Our apartment/house has sea views, 3 double en-suite bedrooms, a living room and fully equipped kitchen and dining room. The three double en-suite bedrooms sleeps 6 comfortably. 

There are 4 balconies with sea, mountain, and poolside views!

Turunc itself, is a very family friendly fishing village with many attractions including a sandy beach, great restaurants, cafes, and internet cafes. It is ideal for families as it very safe. You can have a very nice meal with drinks from£5 -10 per head.

The walk to the beach 0.5km! 10-15 mins. a very pleasant stroll!
